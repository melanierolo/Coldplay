About

Coldplay is a British rock band formed in London in 1996.The group comprises Chris Martin as the lead vocalist and pianist, Jonny Buckland as the guitarist, Guy Berryman as the bassist, and Will Champion as the drummer.Coldplay has become one of the most successful bands in the world, known for their emotive and anthemic rock songs, as well as their dynamic live performances.

Three notable achievements of Coldplay are:

  • MTV Video Music Award for "Best Rock Video" in 2008 for their single "Viva la Vida".
  • Grammy Award for "Record of the Year" in 2004 for their hit single "Clocks".
  • Coldplay has sold over 100 million records worldwide, making them one of the best-selling music artists of all time.
